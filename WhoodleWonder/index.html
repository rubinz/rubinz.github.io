About Whoodles

The Whoodle is a delightful cross between a Soft-coated Wheaten Terrier and a Standard Poodle. The result is an even-tempered, very intelligent, friendly and playful companion. Whoodles are non-shedding, truly hypo-allergenic dogs with softly curling hair and happy, sweet dispositions. Currently accepting deposits for pups from the litter born 12/6/15. Ready for their forever homes January 30th, 2016. Price: $1,200 includes first shots, worming, and vet health certification. Please contact for more information.
